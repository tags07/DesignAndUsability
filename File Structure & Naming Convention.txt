Please stick to this naming conventions:

 - Folders (standard):
   First letter of a word in uppercase, whitespaces between words e.g. 'Pocket Code'

 - Folders (abbreviation):
   All letters uppercase e.g. 'PNG'


 - Files (application files) e.g. '.psd', '.ai': 
   First letter of a word in uppercase, whitespaces between words, logical seperator '-' e.g. '1h Bricks - No Plug & Slot.psd'

 - Files (resource files) e.g. '.jpg', '.png':
   All letters lowercase, '_' (underscore) between words e.g. '1h_brick_no_plug_slot.png'
